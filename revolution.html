Announcing The Revolution: Short Film

Announcement

  • 8 January 2024

In an exciting development for fans and followers, Darius Thomas Productions has officially announced the filming date for its highly anticipated project, "The Revolution: Short Film." Mark your calendars, as the cameras will start rolling on the 23rd of February 2024.

This milestone marks a significant step forward for Darius Thomas Productions, a company known for its commitment to delivering engaging and thought-provoking content. The unveiling of the filming date adds an air of anticipation, inviting audiences to be part of the journey from conception to creation.

Darius Thomas, the creative force behind the production, expressed enthusiasm about reaching this stage of the filmmaking process. "The Revolution: Short Film is a project close to my heart, and I'm thrilled to share that we have a set date for filming. It's a crucial moment in the project's timeline, and I can't wait for our supporters to witness the magic we're creating on screen," Thomas shared.

For those who have been steadfastly following Darius Thomas Productions, this announcement is a call to action. Your support has been integral to the project's evolution, and now, as the filming date approaches, your anticipation becomes a driving force behind the team's efforts.

Stay tuned for behind-the-scenes glimpses, exclusive interviews, and updates as the production progresses towards its final form. Darius Thomas Productions is not just making a film; it's inviting its audience to be part of an experience that goes beyond the screen.

As the countdown to 2/23/24 begins, the stage is set for "The Revolution: Short Film" to captivate hearts and minds. Get ready to be part of something revolutionary.
