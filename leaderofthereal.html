SELF LOVE IS A MEDICINE

  • 17 February 2024

Embracing Beauty, Empowering Souls

This past weekend, Atlanta Utility Works played host to a spectacular event - My Black Is Beautiful Sunday Showcase. There was an amazing performance by the dynamic Peace LVE Gang (PLG). The event was nothing short of magical, bringing together the community in celebration of beauty, culture, and empowerment.

The Venue: Atlanta Utility Works

Nestled in the heart of Atlanta, the industrial-chic backdrop of Atlanta Utility Works provided the perfect canvas for a day filled with creativity, style, and inspiration. The vibrant atmosphere set the stage for a memorable experience.

My Black Is Beautiful Sunday

A beacon of community strength and pride, My Black Is Beautiful Sunday took center stage, showcasing a curated selection of products from local Black-owned businesses. Attendees immersed themselves in a shopping experience that not only celebrated individuality but also supported the growth of the community.

Meet the Peace LVE Gang (PLG)

One of the act performing during this transformative event were the charismatic Peace LVE Gang - Leader of The Real and Master Shaolin. The PLG, abbreviated for Peace LVE Gang, embodies unity, positivity, and empowerment. Through their leadership, they brought an energy that resonated with every participant, leaving a lasting impact on the hearts of all present.

Relive the Magic: Watch the Video Here

To capture the essence of My Black Is Beautiful Sunday and the Peace LVE Gang, we invite you to relive the magic through our exclusive event video. Press play, and immerse yourself in the vibrant moments, empowering messages, and the sheer joy that filled the air.

Join the Movement

As the echoes of My Black Is Beautiful Sunday linger, let's continue the celebration of beauty, culture, and community. Share this blog, spread the love, and join the movement with the Peace LVE Gang.

Thank you to Atlanta Utility Works, My Black Is Beautiful Sunday, and the Peace LVE Gang for an unforgettable experience. Until the next celebration of unity and empowerment!
